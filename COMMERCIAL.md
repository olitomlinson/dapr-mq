# daprMQ Commercial Licensing

## When do you need a commercial license?

A commercial license is required if you intend to:

- Offer daprMQ as a **service to third parties** (SaaS, managed service)
- Include daprMQ in a **paid product or platform**
- Build or sell a **competing messaging or eventing solution**
- Distribute daprMQ as part of a **commercial offering**

---

## What does a commercial license provide?

A commercial license grants you the right to:

- Use daprMQ in **customer-facing products and services**
- Offer daprMQ as a **hosted or managed service**
- Embed daprMQ in **commercial applications**
- Operate **without the restrictions** of the source-available license

---

## Typical use cases

You likely need a commercial license if you are:

- A SaaS provider offering messaging infrastructure  
- A platform company embedding daprMQ into a paid product  
- A cloud provider or MSP hosting daprMQ for customers  
- Building a commercial alternative to daprMQ
